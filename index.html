About

Hi, I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Beihang University (BUAA). As a member of the Fluid and Acoustic Engineering Laboratory, I am supervised by Prof. Lin Du and am also fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Weiguang Zhang.

Prior to this, I obtained my B.Eng. in Aerospace Engineering from the Department of Mechanics and Aerospace Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). During my undergraduate studies, I joined the Advanced Aerodynamics and Aeroacoustics Laboratory (A3 Lab), where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Yu Liu.

My current research focuses on acoustic engineering, noise control, and duct acoustics.
